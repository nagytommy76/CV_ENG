Computer Store webshop | GitHub

Currently working on this hobby project, which is a MERN stack application (MongoDB, Express.Js, React, Node.js). It is a webshop selling computer parts. The user can log in to the application after registration and email confirmation. They can then buy several products, such as CPUs, VGAs, HDDs, and RAMs (I plan to expand the list of products later).

After entering the personal data and selecting the shipping method, the products in the user's cart can be paid with a credit card or with cash. After ordering a summary email will be sent to the users' email address.
